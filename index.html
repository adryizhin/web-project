Senjumaru Shutara

Senjumaru has the appearance of a slender and very youthful woman with fair skin and long, black hair that is adorned at the back by a large golden ornament in the shape of a crescent moon with numerous small lines radiating outwards.

Senjumaru Shutara (修多羅 千手丸, Shutara Senjumaru) is a member of the Royal Guard, holding the title "Great Weave Guard" (大織守, Ōorigami). She holds the position of Fourth Officer of the Zero Division (零番隊 第四官, Zerobantai Daiyonkai), as well as "Divine General of the North" (北方神将, Hoppō Shinshō).
